Certifications, Accreditations and Awards

Here you can list any certifications, accreditations or awards you have received; eg: Chartered Accountant, Registered Nurse, or First Aid Certificate. You are also advised to list any achievements that you may have got whilst at work or at university (sports/uni prizes/employee recognition etc)

Click ‘Add’ to include a new entry. Use ‘Cancel’ to close the form without saving your entry. If you have more than one entry, use the arrows to order them as you choose.

Note: It is important to save each new entry using the button at the foot of the form.
