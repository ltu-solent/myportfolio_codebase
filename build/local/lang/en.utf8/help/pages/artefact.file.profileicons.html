Profile Pictures

What do I do here?

The profile pictures area allows you to upload images that will appear beside your name in many places in the system. Your default image will be shown to other people beside your name around myPortfolio.

How to do it

Use the functionality on this page to upload your profile picture. You may have up to 5 pictures in your profile pictures area at once. Pictures you upload must be at least 16x16 pixels and no greater than 1024x1024 pixels. The images you upload count towards your file quota.
