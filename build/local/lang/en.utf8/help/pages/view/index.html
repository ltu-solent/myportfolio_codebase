Pages

A page contains a selection of artefacts which you arrange and present to others. These may include text boxes, images, video and audio files, journals, CV details, plans, etc. This will all be private until you share the page or provide someone else with a secret URL.
Click the ‘Create page’ button to start a new page from scratch. You can provide a title and description and then click the ‘Save’ button. To edit, click the ‘pencil’ icon whilst in ‘Pages’ or click the ‘Edit this page’ when you are on the actual page (top-right).
See more detailed 'Page' instructions here: Exploring pages.
