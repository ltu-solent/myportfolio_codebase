CV Builder

This section is where you fill in your CV information such as your employment history, work experience, skills and qualifications. You also have the option of uploading files of evidence such as certificates and references to each item.

Click on the 'add' and 'edit' icons and follow the instructions on screen. The 'Employability and Enterprise' team have a great support site that can help you to create great content. See their Skills page and their CV page. If you get stuck, look for the icons as these will help you.
