Find groups

Here you will see groups and get basic information about them. If a group administrator decided to hide a group, you will not be able to see it unless you have been invited to it.
Search for a certain group using a word/words from its title or description. You can become a member of an open group; click ‘Join this group’. If your tutor asks you to join a specific group, search for the name and click ‘Request to join this group’ and your tutor (group administrator) will need to accept this request.
