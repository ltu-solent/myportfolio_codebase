My Groups

Anyone in myPortfolio can create a group for whatever purpose they want; study group, special interest group, or for a project. Within groups you can work on pages together, discuss topics in your own forum, and store files. Your groups can also be private invites only, or open to anyone at Solent. On this page you'll see a list of groups that you're part of and button that allows you to create a group.
Use the filter drop-down list to refine your search terms. To join groups you will need to go to the Find Groups page. To create a group you will need to press the 'Create group' button on the right.
