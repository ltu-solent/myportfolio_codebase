Plans

Plans are simple ‘To Do’ lists. A plan can consist of a number of individual activities with which completion dates are associated. For example, you could create a career plan to help you map out what you want to achieve by which date before you leave University.

Click on the 'New Plan' button to create a new plan, then add a new task to it. Once you have ‘achieved’ the item on your list, you can go back into it and tick it off. This will all be private until you display them on a page and then publish or share that page.
