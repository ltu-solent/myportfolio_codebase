Files

What do I do here?

This is your files area where you can upload your documents either for display purposes or for safe-keeping. You initially have 200MB of space, but this can be increased to 1GB on request (Contact ltu@solent.ac.uk).

How to do it

You have the choice of uploading a file or creating a folder to upload a file into. Folders can help keep this area tidy and is recommended. When uploading, either drag and drop into the indicated area or click 'browse' and select the files you want to upload. You can upload multiple files in one go.
